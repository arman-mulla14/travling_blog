A Journey to the Roof of the World

Ladakh, meaning "land of high passes," is a region administered by India as a union territory. It extends from the Kunlun mountains to the north to the main Great Himalayas to the south. It is known for its remote mountain beauty and distinct culture. Start your adventure today.
